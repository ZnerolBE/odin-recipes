Air Fryer Scrambled Eggs On The Go by Odin Recipes

Air Fryer Scrambled Eggs On The Go by Odin Recipes

This simple air fryer eggs on the go recipe allows you to have a warm protein-packed meal to kick-start your day while your coffee is brewing. I like to place 2 fully cooked turkey sausage links in the basket alongside the ramekin for an added boost of energy.

Recipe by Soup Lovin Nicole

Updated on November 4, 2022

  • Prep Time: 4 mins
  • Cook Time: 6 mins
  • Total Time: 10 mins
Ingredients
  • cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
Directions
  1. Step 1: Lightly spray a ramekin dish with non-stick cooking spray. Pour in beaten eggs.
  2. Step 2: Set air fryer to 370 degrees F (185 degrees C) and place the ramekin into the air fryer basket.
  3. Step 3: Air fry for 3 minutes. Stir egg mixture and cook for 3 minutes more. Fluff with a fork and season to taste with salt and pepper. Grab and go.
